Micro Metronome

Micro Metronome is a minimalistic app, focused on the core functionality of a metronome.

Pick a sound you like, choose your tempo & start playing. Easy to use & lightweight.

Available for iPhone, iPad & Mac.

Red Zebra

Red Zebra is a simple text editor for iPhone & iPad.

It is fully integrated with iCloud & supports a wide variety of raw text file formats - from simple '.txt', to various programming language specific ones (like '.swift', '.c' or '.rs')

It also features some utilities, like word count, text zalgoification, strike-through text & more.

About me

I am a programmer and a musician based in Prague.

I studied classical guitar at Prague Conservatory and bass guitar at Jaroslav Ježek Conservatory. After my studies, I started teaching classical & bass guitar at ZUŠ Vlašim. That's when I picked up coding as a hobby. At first, I was only curious about how computers work at a conceptual level, but after a few weeks, I discovered programming and got immediately hooked!

After learning the basics for a few months, YouTube decided to recommend me Sean Allen's Swift & iOS tutorials. I already had an iPhone & an iPad and I really liked them, so I decided to give making apps for iOS a try. Thanks to my mom, I got my first MacBook Pro, downloaded Xcode and started on a path, that brought me to where I am now.

I've been programming for 3 years and I'm currently looking for a job in iOS development. I would like to join a team that will help me learn and grow - both as a programmer, and as a person. If you're looking for a passionate programmer, get in touch with me via email.
