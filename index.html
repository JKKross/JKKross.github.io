Micro Metronome

Micro Metronome is a minimalistic app, focused on the core functionality of a metronome.

Pick a sound you like, choose your tempo & start playing. Easy to use & lightweight.

Available for iPhone, iPad & Mac.

Red Zebra

Red Zebra is a simple text editor for iPhone & iPad.

It is fully integrated with iCloud & supports a wide variety of raw text file formats - from simple '.txt', to various programming language specific ones (like '.swift', '.c' or '.rs')

It also features some utilities, like word count, text zalgoification, strike-through text & more.
